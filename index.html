Our Story

Mama & Co. started in 2020 as a passionate home-based cloud kitchen during the pandemic. What began as a small passion project quickly grew through word-of-mouth and social media recommendations.

Today, we've evolved into a multi-brand operation with concepts like Ben & Mama and 15th Street Kitchen, partnering with beloved brands and delivery platforms to bring premium cakes and pastries to your doorstep.

Every product is crafted with love, premium ingredients, and a commitment to excellence that defines Mama & Co.
